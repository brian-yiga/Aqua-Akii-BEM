BALANCING SWIMMING AND SCHOOL

The Aqua Akii Swimming Club in Kampala, Uganda, is committed to promoting aquatic activities and fostering a sense of community among swimmers of all ages and skill levels. With a focus on providing top-notch coaching and training facilities, the club offers specialized programs for both competitive swimmers and recreational enthusiasts.

Our Partners

Meet our partners at Aqua Akii Swimming Club. Together, we provide top-tier swimming facilities and work with their skilled coaches to boost our strength and conditioning programs.

We partner with The North Green School to craft a balanced swim and academics program.

We partner with Elite Swim and Gym as our swimming location and with the coaches for strength and conditioning.
