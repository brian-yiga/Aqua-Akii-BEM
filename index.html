About Us

WHO WE ARE

The Aqua Akii Swimming Club in Kampala, Uganda, is committed to promoting aquatic activities and fostering a sense of community among swimmers of all ages and skill levels. With a focus on providing top-notch coaching and training facilities, the club offers specialized programs for both competitive swimmers and recreational enthusiasts.

OUR SQUADS

Our swimmers are split into four training squads. Each squad has its own entry requirements, coaches, performance mentors, and schedules customized to their skills and goals.

HOW WE TRAIN

Join Aqua Akii's diverse swimming program with various skill levels and objectives. Swimmers are expected to meet squad expectations, but there are exceptions in severe cases.
